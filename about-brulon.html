About Brulon Group

With 60 years of expertise in dentistry, Brulon Group stands as a market leader in manufacturing & distribution of top-quality dental materials.

Founded in 1957 by Late Mr. Tilak Raj Seth with its first business unit in the commercial capital of India, New Delhi; Brulon Group kickstart its own manufacturing of artificial acrylic teeth in 1990.

Today, Brulon is a market leader in manufacturing and distribution of various dental materials. It’s a name that resonates with quality & assurance.
