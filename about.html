About PrintoGifts

PrintoGifts, formerly known as Guru Enterprise, was founded by Mr Ashok Patel in 2014. It has since established itself as a leading provider of printing services and gifting in the public and corporate domain.

Today PrintOGifts is recognised for its quality, timely delivery and competitive price. The company has a rich line of printing and gifting solutions that continues to work on the objective of redefining the respective industries, be it attractively mass or ardent bespoke, fulfilling the needs of everyone, thus forming the formidable pillars of PrintOGifts.

Our commitment to Integrity, Excellence and Passion for the work continues to set new benchmarks in the gifting industry.

About the Founder Mr Ashok Patel

Mr Ashok Patel is an entrepreneur whose conviction that the best quality of products will always have a buyer has held true as demonstrated by the steady rise of PrintOGifts.

His attention to detail and customer-centric approach has helped him carve out a special place in the respective fields. The timeliness that has become a trademark has been achieved by focusing on the processes and management that has minimized delays and has accentuated efficiencies.

Mr Patel has a keen insight into the printing and gifting lifecycle, so he ensured that the processes are as hassle-free as possible, right from choosing the right solutions, products, quality, value, etc., to making sure it is delivered on time. The fact that is often neglected is that printing and gifting is not just a thing that is given, but it has reputation, purpose, emotions, appreciations, motivation, values, attitudes, recognition etc. associated with it that plays a pivotal part in cementing a thriving relationship or association. Therein, the procedures at PrintOGifts are custom-tailored keeping in mind these important factors for a seamless and pleasing experience.
