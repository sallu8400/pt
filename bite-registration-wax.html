Product Overview

To deliver a reliable and dimensionally stable wax bite registration you need a material carefully designed for this purpose. Simply warming up a piece of modelling wax, bending it into a U shape and asking a patient to bite down is not cost effective in the long term. Therefore, Brulon offers a proven cost efficient bite registration material, especially formulated to provide dentists with a dense yet easily softened bite wax that is stable at room temperature. The thickness has been carefully chosen to provide the most accurate registration.
