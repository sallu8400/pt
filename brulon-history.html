Company Brulon History
1990

Brulon decided to start its own manufacturing of artificial resin teeth and the unit was established in New Delhi, the capital of India.

2000

Brulon decided to start its own manufacturing of artificial resin teeth and the unit was established in New Delhi, the capital of India.

2003

Production of Perilon V XL - first artificial acrylic teeth & standard shade guide. Brulon Classic A1-D4.

2005

Introduction of Perirock G XL - first acrylic teeth with dentine/enamel layering.

2008

Introduction of Primapan XL - Diversification of technology

2011

R&D of Biorock VXL - new generation of tooth molds in two chromatic layers.

2012

Introduction of Biorock VXL - Best seller tooth of Brulon Teeth range in Asia, North Africa and many European countries.

2013

Increased production efficiency of Biorock VXL, to cope up with the rise in demand.

Introduction of Peristar VXL - first Brulon tooth in three chromatic layers with increased chemical and physical properties.

2014

Introduction of Perilux RXL - acrylic tooth in three chromatic layers with an especially natural appearance & exceptional quality standards.

Introduction of Periceram, first Brulon ceramic tooth (with alloy pin) in two chromatic layers.

2015

A year of comprehensive development & innovation through continuous R&D.

Introduction of Periplus XL - acrylic tooth in multi chromatic layers.

Introduction of Dravlon Heat Cure & Cold Cure Powder/Liquid - High performance Denture Base Resin Materials.

Introduction of Zavlon C & C16 – for the fabrication of all acrylic Crowns & Bridges and prosthesis.

Introduction of AqualSeal – insulating fluid that optimally isolates all Brulon Denture Base Materials.
