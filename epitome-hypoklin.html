Description / Indications

Epitome Hypoklin is used as bactericidal, styptic, and whitening material for medical treatment of root canals, for canal chemical expansion in combination with EDTA, and for tooth cavity disinfection. The stabilized formulation dissolves dentine organic basis, both alive and necrotic tissues as well.

Applicability

  • For disinfection of root canal
  • To irrigate canal after filling with appropriate chelating agent
