Key features / benefits

  • High durability. Thanks to excellent resistance to abrasion due to double crosslinking.
  • Unique golden proportion of enamel-dentine. Thus increasing the translucent effect.
  • Reliable color stability.
  • Substantial resistance to plaque adhesion.
  • Ideal choice for dentures with life-like effect.
  • Optimum bonding characteristics with DravlonTM Denture Base Resins.

Product Overview

Premium acrylic teeth fabricated from Polymethyl methacrylate with 4 chromatic layers. Thanks to its innovative multifunctional characteristic for natural aesthetics, it inherents natural shade effect with excellent shade accuracy to the shade guide.

Available Modules

  • 15 tooth moulds of upper anteriors (divided in three groups).
  • 7 tooth moulds of lower anteriors.
  • 5 sets of upper/ lower posteriors.
  • Product kits available only in sets of 28 teeth.

Shades available in 6 Brulon Classic shades (A1, A2, A3, A3.5, B1, B2).

Further shades are subject to delivery time.
